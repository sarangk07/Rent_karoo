Insurance and Damage Policy

Silver plan

- Covers damage to the rental vehicle caused by collision, theft, or vandalism.
- May have a deductible, which is the amount the customer is responsible for paying before the insurance coverage kicks in.
- Typically the most affordable option

Gold plan

- Provides more comprehensive coverage than the basic plan, including damage to the rental vehicle caused by natural disasters, riots, or acts of terrorism.
- May also cover personal belongings of the customer that are damaged or stolen while in the rental vehicle.
- Typically more expensive than the basic plan.

Diamond plan

- Provides 24/7 roadside assistance, including towing, flat tire repair, fuel delivery, and lockout assistance.
- May also include trip interruption coverage, which reimburses the customer for expenses incurred if their trip is interrupted due to a covered event.
- Typically offered as an optional add-on to the basic or premium plan.
